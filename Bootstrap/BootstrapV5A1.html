India's population in 2025 is estimated to be 1.46 billion (1,463,865,525) people, making it the most populous country in the world, according to Worldometer. This figure is based on the United Nations' mid-year estimate.

China's population in 2025 is projected to be around 1.416 billion people. This represents a slight decrease from the previous year and reflects a trend of demographic shifts, including a declining birth rate.

The projected U.S. population for July 1, 2025, is 347,275,807. This figure represents approximately 4.22% of the global population.
